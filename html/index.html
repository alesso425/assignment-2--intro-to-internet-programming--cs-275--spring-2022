Rice Recipes by Alessandro DiCesare

Making Sprouted California Rice:

For slightly al dente rice:

Combine cups of rice with cups of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice:

Increase liquid by 1/2 cup and cook time by 5 minutes.

Making White Rice:

Combine cup of rice with cups of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting. Cook for about 18 minutes.
